Our Mission

The International Society for Universal Dialogue, or ISUD for short, is a society for the betterment of our world.

Who are we?

We are a society mainly composed of scholars and philosophers from all over the world. We are devoted to promoting international dialogue, fostering discussions relating to fundamental questions of universal concern. Questions whose answers affect all humanity.

What do we ask?

As we push international dialogue forward with fundamental questions, we could not do so without said questions.

  • What role does Philosophical thinking have to play in moving forward toward a more rational and more human future?
  • What are ethical principles of dialogue?
  • How may one understand cultural diversity and universality?
  • How best to work toward a culture of peace?
  • What is the relationship between the basic liberties of individuals and the collective rights of cultural groups?

These are just some questions we ask not only ourselves, but the world at large. We believe that by following these questions to their rational answers and conclusions we will be able to push for a betterment of our entire international community as one.
